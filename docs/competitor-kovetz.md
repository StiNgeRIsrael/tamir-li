## Comparison snapshot

| Dimension | Tamir.li | Kovetz.co.il |

| Focus | Multi-format (image, video, audio, PDF, AI) | PDF-only (~33 tools) |
| Hebrew UI | Native RTL | Native |

## Where Kovetz wins

- PDF editor, forms, redact, nikud, deep PDF toolbox
- Pro OCR quality (Google Document AI)
- Israeli card payments via Grow

## Where Tamir.li wins

- Images, video, audio, AI image generation
- Higher free/Premium file caps (50/500 MB vs 25/100 MB)
- 5/day vs 3/hour free quota — market explicitly on upsell
- Free Hebrew OCR wedge vs Kovetz Pro-only Doc AI
- WhatsApp video compress, HEIC iPhone, audio conversion landings
